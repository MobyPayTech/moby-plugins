## Overview

The MobyPayCheckoutGateway plugin integrates Moby Checkout as a payment gateway for Shopify stores, offering a seamless and secure checkout experience for customers. With this plugin, Shopify merchants can easily add MobyPay as a payment option, enhancing the payment process and improving customer satisfaction.

---

## Requirements

- **Shopify Account:** An active Shopify store with administrator access.
- **MobyPay Account:** A valid MobyPay account with onboarding completed. Upon onboarding, you will receive an **API Key** and **Secret Key**.

---

## Installation

### Step 1: Install the Plugin

1. Go to the [MobyPay Shopify Plugin page](https://apps.shopify.com/moby-checkout) on the Shopify App Store.
2. Click **Add app** and follow the prompts to install the plugin on your Shopify store.

### Step 2: Configure the Plugin

1. Once installed, navigate to **Settings > Payments** in your Shopify admin panel.
2. In the **Alternative Payment Methods** section, select **MobyPay** as your payment provider.
3. Enter your **API Key** and **Secret Key** from your MobyPay account. These are the credentials provided to you during the MobyPay onboarding process.
4. Configure additional settings:
   - **Environment:** Select **Test** for sandbox testing or **Live** for production.
   - **Transaction Description:** Optional description shown to customers.
5. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

> ⚠️ Always test your integration in **Test** mode before switching to **Live**.

---

## Upgrading

When a new version of the plugin is available on the Shopify App Store:

1. Shopify will notify you of available updates in your admin panel under **Apps**.
2. Review the update notes, then click **Update** to install the latest version.
3. After upgrading, navigate to **Settings > Payments > MobyPay** to verify your credentials and settings are still correctly configured.

---

## Usage

After configuring, **MobyPay** will appear as a payment option during checkout. Customers can select MobyPay to complete their purchase, and transactions will be securely processed through the MobyPay gateway.
